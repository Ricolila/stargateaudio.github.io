Stargate DAW is a new digital audio workstation with a powerful pattern based workflow ideal for electronic music, and comprehensive suite of built in plugins. It has highly CPU optimized algorithms that have been tested to work well on even an old Core2 Duo laptop running large projects with many plugins.

Stargate DAW has many innovative features for mobility and portability. The project folders can be easily moved between computers, it can run on a USB flash drive alongside your projects and sample libraries, and provides excellent battery life when used on a laptop.

Learn more at the official website or download from Github

Stargate DAW is a new digital audio workstation with a focus on strong cross platform support, highly CPU optimized algorithms and an innovative, integrated experience. It has been tested to scale to at least 16 cores, and work well on low end hardware such as AMD Trinity and Core2 Duo laptops, or Raspberry Pi4, even running large projects with many instances of the built-in plugins.
